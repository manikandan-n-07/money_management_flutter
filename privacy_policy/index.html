1. Overview

Welcome to Cashier, your personal offline expense management app developed by Manilunar. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use Cashier on your Android or Windows device.

Cashier is designed to be a fully offline-first application. All of your financial records, budgets, categories, and settings are stored entirely on your device and are never transmitted to any external server or cloud service operated by us.

✅
100% Offline

Cashier works completely without an internet connection. No data leaves your device.

📦

2. Information We Collect

Cashier is designed with a minimal data footprint. We collect the following information locally on your device only:

💰 Expense Records

Amounts, categories, notes, and dates you enter manually.

👤 User Name

The display name you optionally set inside the app.

⚙️ App Preferences

Currency, theme (dark/light), font, and notification settings.

📊 Budget Data

Budget limits and categories you define within the app.

🤝 Split Records

Bill-splitting entries and participant names you add.

🔔 Notification Schedule

Your configured notification times and preferences.

ℹ️
All Data Stays Local

All of the above data is stored locally using the Hive database on your device and is never uploaded, synced, or shared with any server.

🚫

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be crystal clear about what we never collect:

  • ❌ Personal identification information (email, phone number, government ID)
  • ❌ Location or GPS data
  • ❌ Device identifiers, IMEI, or advertising IDs
  • ❌ Contacts, SMS messages, or call logs
  • ❌ Bank account or credit card details
  • ❌ Photos, camera, or microphone data
  • ❌ Browsing history or usage analytics
  • ❌ Crash reports sent to our servers
  • ❌ Any biometric data
💾

4. Local Data Storage

Cashier uses the Hive local database and Shared Preferences to store all your data on your device. This means:

  • Your data is stored in the app's private sandbox on your device.
  • Uninstalling Cashier will permanently delete all your expense data from your device.
  • If you use the Export feature (PDF/Excel), the resulting file is saved to a location of your choice on your device and is your responsibility.
  • If you use the Share feature, you are choosing to share that specific exported file — no automatic background sharing occurs.
  • Data backed up through Android's native backup service is governed by Google's privacy policy, not ours.
🔑

5. App Permissions

Cashier may request the following permissions. Each is optional and used only for the specific feature described:

Permission Purpose Required?
🔔 Notifications Send daily expense reminder notifications Optional
📁 Storage (Read/Write) Save exported PDF or Excel files to your Downloads folder Optional
📤 Share Share exported reports via other apps on your device Optional

We do not request internet, location, contacts, camera, or microphone permissions.

🔗

6. Third-Party Services

Cashier does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or social media platforms. The following open-source libraries are used internally but do not collect or transmit any data:

  • Hive — Local key-value database (offline only)
  • Flutter Riverpod — State management (no network calls)
  • FL Chart — Chart rendering (no network calls)
  • Poppins (Google Fonts) — May load font files from Google's CDN on first launch (internet-optional)
  • flutter_local_notifications — Local device notifications only (no server)
⚠️
Google Fonts Note

If you use the app with an internet connection, Google Fonts may be loaded from Google's servers. This is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. Font files are cached locally after first download.

🛡️

7. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your data depends on the security of your device itself. We recommend:

  • Using a strong screen lock (PIN, pattern, fingerprint) on your device.
  • Keeping your Android OS and Cashier app updated to the latest version.
  • Being careful about who has physical access to your device.
  • Regularly exporting your data as a backup if needed.

Since we never receive your data, we cannot be held responsible for any breach originating from your device's security being compromised.

👶

8. Children's Privacy

Cashier is not directed at children under the age of 13. Since we do not collect any personal data from any users whatsoever, there is no risk of us knowingly collecting data from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has used this app, please note that all data is local to their device and can be removed by uninstalling the app.
